Genesis is a secondary server for Utopia. Commonly used for testing new and exiting game mechanics, Genesis is a fast-paced, high intensity server with fast ticks that range from 5 minutes to 15 minutes per tick. Each age lasts anywhere between 3 and 10 days. Do you have what it takes to survive with the super intense environment?

=== Differences ===
There are several notable differences between the Genesis and [[WoL]] servers:
\* Significantly smaller kingdom size
\* '''Assassinate Wizards''' has been renamed to '''Subdue Wizards'''. Instead of an Instant Damage Op, it is a Sabotage Op that reduces Wizard Efficiency
\* Affluent Ritual now has Wizard Production
\* '''ALL''' races are unable to train elites. Elites are produced tickly from peasants and specialists
\* Ticks are usually faster (usually 15 minutes; may vary between 2 and 30 minutes)
\* Attack, Train and Build times are usually halved
\* Resource generation is usually (greatly) increased
\* Several thresholds for mechanics are raised
