## What is Metatron Plus?

**Developer** celien

Also known as **Metatron**.

Metatron Plus is an Add-on for [Mozilla Firefox](Mozilla_Firefox.md) especially created for [Utopia](Utopia.md). It formats the Utopia pages and improves the general interface.

It may be downloaded at https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/9653 - Not supported on new code yet

Metatron Team recommend using [/index.php/UTools](UTools.md) insted, a simulair addon wich is more activily developed at this point.

## Requirements

- A Mozilla Firefox Browser

## Features of Metatron Plus

| **Features List** | | |
| --- | --- | --- |
| - Ability to Redo spells and ops multiple times on the same province without having to re-select anything.  - Kingdom Pages may be sorted by PROVINCE NAME / NETWORTH / LAND / NWPA or HONOR.  - Throne Page is formatted by TPA, WPA, OPA, DPA [1].  - All locations are clickable and will bring you to their kingdom page.  - Ability to Bookmark & Comment on Kingdoms for easy selection on the Mystics, Thievery, War Room, Message & Kingdom Page.  - Ability to Mass Message your Kingdom.  - War Room Calculator.  - Summarizes Kingdom Pages. |
