# Gold Status

## Gold Status

This item gives your account Gold status, providing your master account access to a wealth of special benefits!

- Advertisement free gameplay
- Hostility meter
- Access to Mentor other provinces
- Three Sitter Credits (Utopia Gold status)
- Seven Sitter Credits (Utopia Gold status 75 Days)

### Cost

- 1 Month - 15 Credits
- 3 Months - 35 Credits
- 75 Days - 35 Credits

## Kingdom Gold

In the shop located under, Utopia Gold Status, you may purchase 25 gold tokens at once!

- 75 Days x 25 - 600 Credits
