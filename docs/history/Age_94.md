## Mechanics

### Race System

- Primary Race pick determines the Base Military Units listed in each race entry
- Spell lists are combined
- Elite values are determined by the primary pick base elite plus the secondary pick adjustment
- Secondary pick does not grant the primary racial bonus
- Only the penalties of the primary Race pick apply
- Half-blooded pairings get no innate bonus for going half-blood
- Same-race pairings get an innate bonus for picking the same race twice: Town Watch

### Examples

**Bonuses:**

- +1 General
- -15% Attack Time
- +35% Birth Rate
- -30% Military Losses
- Spreads and is Immune to the Plague
- Start with +200 Elites, +200 Soldiers, and +200 Specialist Credits

**Penalties:**

- No Access to Stables and War Horses

**Spell Book:**

- Clear Sight
- Ghost Workers

**Units:**

- Soldier: 2/2, 1.0 NW
- Offensive Specialist: 12/0, 4.8 NW
- Defensive Specialist: 0/10, 5.0 NW
- Elite Unit: 15/5, 1500gc, 8.0 NW
- Mercenary: 6/0, 0.0 NW
- Prisoner: 6/0, 1.2 NW
- War Horse: n/a

#### Human, True Blooded

**Bonuses:**

- +10% Science Effectiveness
- +21% Income
- 51% Protection from Learns (-51% science lost when learned)
- Each Acre holds 2 Prisoners
- Start with 5000 Science Books in each Category

**Penalties:**

**Spell Book:**

- Hero's Inspiration
- Fountain of Knowledge
- Town Watch

**Units:**

- Soldier: 2/2, 1.0 NW
- Offensive Specialist: 10/0, 4.0 NW
- Defensive Specialist: 0/10, 5.0 NW
- Elite Unit: 13/8, 1500gc, 8.25 NW
- Mercenary: 8/0, 0.0 NW
- Prisoner: 8/0, 1.2 NW
- War Horse: 3/0, 0.9 NW

### Military, Attacking & Defending

- Learn Attack no longer captures Science Books in War; instead it reduces effectiveness similar to Amnesia, with base gains of 5% effectiveness
- Massacre Attack damage to peasants increased by 50% in War

### Thievery

- Steal Horses now requires at least Unfriendly

### Magic

- Guile: increases Instant Spell Damage and Sabotage Operation Damage by 15% for the duration, down from 20%
