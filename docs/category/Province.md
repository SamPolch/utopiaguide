# Province

A province is the collection of [acres](/web/20211026155418//index.php?title=Throne#Top_Bar "Throne") to which province is allotted.

### Creation

[Creating a province](/web/20211026155418//index.php?title=Creating_a_province "Creating a province") is easy and free.

### Destruction

A province can be lost or destroyed if it's total acres drop down to 0.

### Abandonment

A province can be [abandoned](/web/20211026155418//index.php?title=Preferences#Abandon_Province "Preferences") but the lands kept intact for a new leader to take over or, in an act of ultimate betrayal, they can be completely destroyed.
